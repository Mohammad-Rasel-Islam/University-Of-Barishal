Admission Requirements:

For Admission in the Evening MBA program, the candidaes, from any discipline, must have:

  1. a four-year bachelor's degree(s), or a three-year bachelor or an equivalent degree(s), and
  2. At least five (05) points calculated on the basis outlined in section 2.2 with not morethan one third class/divisionfor
    all levels of examination.
